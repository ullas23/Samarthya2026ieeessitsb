What is Samarthya?

Samarthya 2026 is the flagship annual technical festival of IEEE SSIT Student Branch. A realm where engineers, innovators, and creators converge to forge their legacies through knowledge, skill, and ingenuity.

ᚢ
The Vision

Celebrating innovation, engineering excellence, and creativity across multiple IEEE technical societies, Samarthya brings together the brightest minds for two days of legendary trials and collaborative discovery.

ᚦ
The Arena

Held at SSIT, Tumakuru on April 22–23, 2026, the festival spans competitive events, technical showcases, and collaborative challenges — each a trial worthy of a true innovator's mettle.
